# Faceless
Faceless MCBE Add-On introduces an undying specter to your worlds — turning them into a truly terrifying and perilous place.

This mob spawns at night in all biomes and is completely immune to damage. To get rid of it, you must flee and stay away for a few minutes. It deals 3 points of damage and teleports randomly.

⚔️ ATK: 3 | ❤️ HP: ∞

This addon is optimized to run effectively on Realms and dedicated servers with minimal hardware resource consumption and its available in english and español. 🇬🇧 🇲🇽
